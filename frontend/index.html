Policy Insights

Key Findings & Recommendations

Data-driven insights to guide agricultural policy, investment priorities, and resource allocation decisions.

⚠️
Groundwater Crisis in NW India
Punjab and Haryana show alarming groundwater depletion due to intensive paddy cultivation. Immediate policy intervention required including crop diversification incentives and water-use efficiency programs.
62m avg well depth, ↑18m in 5 years
📈
Land Fragmentation Accelerating
Average holding size has fallen from 1.8ha (2010) to 1.3ha (2023) due to inheritance subdivision. Cooperative farming and land consolidation policies can improve productivity by 30-40%.
86% farms are <2ha (marginal/small)
💡
Canal Irrigation Underutilized
Only 38% of canal network capacity is currently utilized due to poor maintenance and distribution inefficiencies. Targeted rehabilitation can increase irrigated area by 12 million hectares.
38% canal system capacity used
🌱
Crop Diversification Opportunity
Rice-Wheat monoculture dominates 45% of cropped area, depleting soil nutrients and water. Introducing pulses, oilseeds, and horticulture can improve soil health and farmer income by 25%.
45% area under rice-wheat rotation
💧
Micro-irrigation Scale-up Needed
Drip and sprinkler adoption remains at only 12% of potential area. Scaling micro-irrigation to 50% coverage could reduce agricultural water use by 40% while maintaining or increasing yields.
12% micro-irrigation adoption rate
🗺️
Regional Disparity in Data
Northeast states, tribal belts of Central India, and arid zones of Rajasthan remain data-sparse regions. Mobile data collection campaigns can bridge this gap for better targeted policy making.
23% districts with incomplete data
